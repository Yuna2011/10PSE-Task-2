# My Top Trumps Car Game
Yuna Shin

## Description 
My documentation includes a breakdown of the design, structure and mechanics of my Top Trumps game. It covers every stage of its development, from planning to class design to gameplay rules. 

## My Game
My game is a strategic, object-oriented card game where players compare car attributes, win rounds and manage a shared middle pile to outplay their opponents. The game blends luck, strategy and risk-reward decision-making, supported by a clean OOP class structure.   
It is played with 2 ~ 3 players, each receiving an equal pile of cards.  
In every round:  
- Players flip their top card
- The starting player chooses their strongest attribute
- All players compare the same attribute
- The highest value wins the round
- The winner chooses to either add the cards to the middle pile or give them to the lowest‑scoring player
- The next player becomes the caller for the following round
The game ends when one player loses all their cards, making them the winner.  
Draws send all cards from that round directly into the middle pile.
